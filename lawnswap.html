Lawn Swap (Photoshop)

Lawn Swap is a photo series in which I used Photoshop to swap the lawns between two homes (each pair here being left-right) in order to bring to light any disparities between a homeowner's upkeep (or lack thereof) of their lawn and the rest of their home exterior: plants, cars, or even the house itself. For the sake of making each photo more interesting to look at, additional elements were added to accentuate the feelings of care or neglect given off by the lawns.
