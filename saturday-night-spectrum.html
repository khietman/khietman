Saturday Night Spectrum

There are often times where I'll find myself in a dark room sitting in front of an illuminated screen to play a video game or watch a movie, and as I'm sitting there I'm bombarded by a constant visual cacophany of colored light that's splashing across the room; meanwhile those same lights are also being directed and contentrated into my eyeballs.

Materials: Arduino, Processing, 123D Make, plywood, glass, Xbox 360 controller, RGB LED
