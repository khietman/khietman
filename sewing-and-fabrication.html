Sewing and Fabrication

Sewing and Fabrication served as the conclusion to my term as a Resident Artist at TechShop San Jose as well as a trial run for the concepts that I would later flesh out for my BFA show; in particular, the concept of the 2D cross section revealing an internal image hidden within the layers of the 3D sculpture. The pieces were fabricated using laser cutting and CNC embroidery.

Materials: Plywood, fabric, embroidery thread, 123D Make, Adobe Illustrator, acrylic
